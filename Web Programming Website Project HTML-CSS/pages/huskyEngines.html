Husky Engines
D-Series Trucks

Small Block, Big Bite

The Husky line of small-block engines from Diamond Vehicle Corporation are a line of Inline- and V-Styled, small-block engines that will have more than enough to pull you down the road, trail, or anywhere in-between. These capable engines have been designed for reliability and ease of repair, and built using only high-quality materials that ensure that the Husky engines can handle most things you can throw at them.

Features of the Husky Engines:

  • Multi-Point Electronic Fuel-Injection
  • High-Quality Construction
  • Lightweight Aluminium Engine Block
  • Can run on gasoline, ethonal, aviation gas, propane, and hydrogen. Note: DVC sells conversion kits for propane and hydrogen conversion, but these conversions come at the cost of reduced performance out of the engine.
